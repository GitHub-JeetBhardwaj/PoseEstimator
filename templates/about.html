About Us

Welcome to Pose Detection System, a user-friendly platform designed to simplify human pose detection and management. Our application provides an intuitive interface to interact with pose detection technology, allowing users to add, delete, and recognize human poses in real-time.

Our Features

  • Add a Custom Pose: Save your own unique poses by capturing live frames from your camera and storing the pose details for future recognition.
  • Delete Existing Poses: Manage your custom poses effortlessly by deleting poses you no longer need.
  • Live Pose Detection: Detect and identify human poses in real-time using advanced computer vision algorithms powered by MediaPipe and OpenCV.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Built using Python and Flask for efficient server-side processing.
  • Computer Vision: MediaPipe Pose Estimation and OpenCV for robust pose detection.
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS for a modern and responsive interface.

Developer

This application was developed by Jeet Bhardwaj, a passionate programmer with a keen interest in artificial intelligence and computer vision. The project demonstrates the integration of advanced AI techniques with real-world usability.

Vision

Our goal is to make pose detection technology accessible and easy to use for everyone. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast, a developer, or just curious about computer vision, this platform is built for you!

Feel free to explore the features, experiment with poses, and discover the exciting possibilities of pose detection technology!
